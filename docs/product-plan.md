# GuideRoom Product Plan

## Positioning

GuideRoom helps a guide create a live audio room in about 30 seconds. Visitors scan a QR code and listen with their own phones and earphones.

The initial target users are freelance tour guides, small private tours, school visits, museum or city walk groups, company visits, and temporary group activities.

## Product stages

### Stage 1: MVP live original audio

Goal: validate the core loop.

Core loop:

1. Guide creates a room.
2. Guide gets a QR code.
3. Visitors scan and open the listener page.
4. Guide speaks through the phone microphone.
5. Visitors listen in their mobile browser.

Not included in stage 1:

- AI translation
- subtitles
- native iOS or Android apps
- complex guide accounts
- payment
- organization admin panel
- offline LAN mode

### Stage 2: Paid sessions

Goal: allow small guides to pay per session.

Possible pricing tests:

- CHF 3 for 1 hour
- CHF 5 for up to 3 hours
- CHF 9 for half a day
- CHF 39 for 10 sessions

### Stage 3: Captions and AI translation

Goal: add differentiated value after the live audio flow is validated.

Possible features:

- speech-to-text captions
- translated captions
- translated audio channels
- per-minute or per-session AI add-on pricing

### Stage 4: Organization version

Goal: serve travel agencies, museums, schools, and companies.

Possible features:

- multiple guide accounts
- team management
- branded listener pages
- invoices
- statistics
- parallel rooms
- enterprise subscriptions

## MVP success criteria

Stage 1 is successful when:

- a guide can create a room on a phone
- the system shows a QR code
- 5 to 10 visitors can join from phones
- visitors can hear the guide with acceptable delay
- no visitor app installation is required
- the whole flow is simple enough for ordinary tourists
